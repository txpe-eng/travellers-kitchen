Here at Traveller's Kitchen, we aim to make event preparations easier, more efficient and overall, less time consuming.

Event planning can often be stressful, especially when it comes to organizing food vendors that match the style, budget, and theme of an event. Many event organizers spend a lot of time searching online, contacting vendors individually, and comparing menus and availability.

We strive to act as a bridge between food truck owners and customers. We do this by solving the main problem and this led us to the answer to bringing multiple food trucks together on one easy-to-use platform.

The application allows users to browse available food trucks, explore their menus, check availability for specific dates, and book them directly through the platform.

This saves time and makes the planning process faster and more efficient.

About this truck

C.O.W. (Cheese on Wheels) is a mobile cheese experience that brings the finest artisanal cheeses directly to your event. Our mission is to share our passion for cheese and create unforgettable moments through curated cheese boards, expert pairings, and exceptional service.

Whether you’re hosting a wedding, corporate event, or private party, C.O.W. offers a unique and delicious way to elevate your gathering. Our team of cheese experts will work with you to create a customized cheese board that perfectly complements your event’s theme and your guests’ tastes.

From creamy bries to sharp cheddars, our selection of cheeses is sourced from the best producers around the world. We also offer a variety of accompaniments, including artisanal crackers, fresh fruits, and gourmet spreads, to enhance the cheese-tasting experience.
